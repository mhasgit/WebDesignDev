Our Story

Established in 2015, Eminent Enterprises provided pharmaceutical solutions and consultancy to emerging

industries in the field. The basic focus was on the establishment of new industries and on

helping nascent industries with their initial issues and difficulties. Eminent Enterprises

started as a platform that provided affordable running and maintenance services for

industrial and lab machinery and equipment. Throughout its experience, it was able to achieve

high-quality products and services. Today, in 2021, it has been scaled to a higher level to

accommodate its clients, such as small and medium-sized enterprises and organizations

across Pakistan for industrial services and I.T solutions.

  • Establishment and Implementation of GMP Compliance System and ISO Certification Process in M/S Medwell Pharmaceuticals
  • Consultation For Implementation of Quality Management System in M/S SPL Pharmaceuticals Hattar.
  • Conducted DRAP GMP inspections in Pak Risen Pharmaceuticals Hattar.
  • Implementation of QMS in M/S Filix Pharmaceuticals Rawat.
  • Maintenance work of Incinerator, Low Temprature Refrigirators and other items in NIH Laboratories Islamabad
  • Providing Consultancy for Upcoming GMP inspection for Establishment of premises for import licence to M/S 3D lifestyle Lahore.
